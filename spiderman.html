Friendly Neighborhood Spiderman

This project started as a quick creative warm-up. I used Python’s imageIO library to stitch together hand-drawn frames into a simple GIF, just to get back into coding before classes start.

Keepin it Simple

Working with imageIO reminded me how one library has such expansive potential. This was not a technically advanced project, but just a quick python syntax refresher.

Made with (Spider) Love

Every frame of the gif was hand drawn by me. It slowed the process down quite a bit, but made the result even more rewarding. A great way to combine two of my favorite things.
